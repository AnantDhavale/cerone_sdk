# Data & Privacy

Ceron's free trial processes agent action data to provide runtime validation and trust scoring. By using this SDK with Ceron, you acknowledge:

- What we collect: agent IDs, declared purposes, capability lists, action names, parameters passed to validation, alignment scores, and trust events. We do not collect your model API keys, model outputs, or end-user data unless you explicitly include them in validation parameters.
- How we use it: to provide the validation service, improve semantic alignment models, and detect abuse. Anonymized and aggregated validation patterns may be used to train and improve Ceron's alignment engine.
- What we do not do: we do not sell your data, share it with third parties, or use it to train models that compete with your product.
- Retention: free trial data is retained for 30 days. Paid tier data retention is configurable.
- Your model key stays yours: Ceron never sees or stores your LLM API keys. Ceron sits between your agent and its actions, not between you and your model.

For enterprise data handling, custom retention, or on-premise deployments, contact `info@homersemantics.com`.
